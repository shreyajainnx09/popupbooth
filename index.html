🔒 Privacy Policy
Overview
Our Photobooth values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the site's practices regarding the collection, use, and handling of data when you use the service.
No Photo Storage
The site does not store your photos on servers. All photo processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos remain on your device and are not uploaded to servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.
Data The Site Doesn't Collect
  • The site does not store the photos you take or upload
  • The site does not collect personally identifiable information
  • The site does not use cookies to track your browsing activity
  • The site does not sell any data to third parties
Analytics
The site may use anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the application. This helps improve the user experience but does not identify individual users.
Your Rights
You have the right to:
  • Access information the site may have about you
  • Request deletion of any data associated with you
  • Opt-out of analytics
Changes to This Policy
The site may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes by the posting of the new Privacy Policy on this page.
